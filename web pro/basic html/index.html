Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji is a famous Indian street food made with a spicy blend of mashed vegetables served with butter-toasted buns. It's rich, flavorful, and perfect for any meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup chopped capsicum
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tsp pav bhaji masala
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté the chopped onions.
  2. Add tomatoes and capsicum; cook until soft.
  3. Mix in the boiled potatoes and peas; mash well.
  4. Add pav bhaji masala and salt; stir and simmer for 5–7 minutes.
  5. Toast pav buns with butter on a flat pan.
  6. Serve hot with lemon wedges and chopped coriander.
